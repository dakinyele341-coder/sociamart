# Privacy Policy

_This is a starting template. Have it reviewed by a qualified lawyer before launch._

This Privacy Policy explains what information SociaMart ("we", "us") collects, how we use
it, and the choices you have. By using SociaMart you agree to the practices described here.

## 1. Information we collect

**Information you give us:**

- Account details: name, phone number, and/or email address.
- Profile and store details: business name, bio, profile photo, WhatsApp number.
- Listings: product titles, descriptions, prices, and images you upload.
- Communications: feedback and messages you send us.

**Information we collect automatically:**

- Approximate or precise location (only with your permission) to show nearby listings.
- Usage and device data, such as pages viewed and interactions, via privacy-respecting
  analytics.

## 2. How we use your information

We use your information to:

- Create and manage your account.
- Show you relevant listings and sellers near your location.
- Connect buyers and sellers (for example, generating WhatsApp links).
- Operate, maintain, and improve the Service.
- Keep the platform safe and prevent fraud and abuse.

## 3. Location data

Location is used to power proximity features — showing items and sellers near you. You can
choose to share your precise location, pick a town manually, or change it at any time.
You can revoke location permission through your browser or device settings.

## 4. How we share information

- **With other users:** your public profile, store details, and listings are visible to
  others. Your WhatsApp number is shared when you choose to be contacted.
- **With service providers:** we use trusted third parties (for example, hosting,
  authentication, and analytics) who process data on our behalf.
- **For legal reasons:** we may disclose information where required by law or to protect
  the rights and safety of our users.

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## 5. Data retention

We keep your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide the
Service. You may request deletion of your account and associated personal data at any time.

## 6. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, or delete your
personal data, or to object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, contact us
using the details below.

## 7. Security

We use reasonable technical and organisational measures, including row-level security and
access controls, to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is
completely secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Children

SociaMart is not intended for anyone under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data
from children.

## 9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will revise the "Last updated" date
and, where appropriate, notify you in the app.

## 10. Contact

For privacy questions or requests, reach us through the in-app feedback form or at
**privacy@sociamart.app**.
